Are mentorship and coaching the same?

Description

They are not the same but similar. Read below to find out how.

Mentorship typically involves a more experienced individual (mentor) guiding and offering advices to a less experienced person often in a broader, long term capacity. While

Coaching on the other hand is a more structured, goal-oriented relationship focused on developing specific skills or achieving specific outcomes.

While both involves guidance and supports, their methods and goals differs.
